Intro

First, please enjoy this watercolored Northern Flicker. This is one of my favorite birds.

I am passionate about community-building, climate action, coding, and creating art.

My 9-5: I am the data manager at the Sitka Sound Science Center (SSSC), a research field station in Sitka, Alaska. I am grateful to work with a wonderful team of community leaders, scientists, and researchers. I create data visualization products and develop and maintain data management infrastructure including the SSSC GitHub Organization. My project team is working on reducing landslide risk in Southeast Alaska through increasing coordination.
