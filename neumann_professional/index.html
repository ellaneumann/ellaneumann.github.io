A glimpse at my technology experience

FIRST Robotics

I was first hooked on technology when I joined my high school robotics team in 2018. In FIRST Robotics, I realized that there was so much opportunity to change the world through technology and lots of work to be done recruiting young women to join the tech field.

Hewlett-Packard

In the summer of 2019, I collaborated with a team to engineer a Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) testing system using semiconductors and C programming. I also developed data visualization software to track the throughput and registered signal strength indices (RSSI) of BLE, allowing for signal optimization.

GHC

Attending Grace Hopper Celebration of Women in computing in the fall of 2020, was life changing. At GHC, I was surrounded by thousands of women who were making an imact through the use of technology and was inspired to continue pursuing a career in computer science.
